File Description:

The files are ingredients extracted from Recipe1M dataset. We preprocessed these ingredients and merged ingredients with similar meanings into one.
Ingredients are merged if they:
• Are close together in Word2vec embedding space, which is trained on the titles, ingredients and instructions of Recipe1M.
• Share two or three words.
• Are mapped to the same item in Nutritionix.

Reference:

[1] Han, Fangda, Ricardo Guerrero, and Vladimir Pavlovic. "CookGAN: Meal Image Synthesis from Ingredients." The IEEE Winter Conference on Applications of Computer Vision. 2020.
[2] Li, Jiatong, Ricardo Guerrero, and Vladimir Pavlovic. "Deep Cooking: Predicting Relative Food Ingredient Amounts from Images." Proceedings of the 5th International Workshop on Multimedia Assisted Dietary Management. 2019.
